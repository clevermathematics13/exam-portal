📋 The Reflection Process

This tool helps you reflect on your exam performance in 3 steps:

1 Self-Grade — Select an exam below and enter the marks you think you earned for each question. Be honest!
2 Compare — Once Clev has graded, come back to see a side-by-side comparison. Your Judgement Disagreement % shows how closely your self-assessment matched. To reach 0%, there are exactly two ways this can happen:

① You gave yourself too many marks. Check the mark scheme, accept that Clev was right, and lower your "You Said" score to match his mark.

② Clev gave you too few marks. Check the mark scheme and find clear evidence that your answer deserves more credit than Clev awarded. You must bring both your original exam paper and the mark scheme to Clev in person. Do not simply raise your "You Said" mark without the evidence — Clev will check. If he agrees with your evidence, he will use the Teacher Override on this page to correct his score on the spot.
3 Upload Corrections — When disagreement reaches 0%, upload a PDF of your corrected work to complete the process.

💡 Tip: Click the title "CleverReflection" to reset. Use the 📊 button above to see your overall mastery by syllabus topic.
